Jack's Hobbies and Achievements

Tchoukball

I am an avid tchoukball player and am proud to say that I have represented my country in several international tournaments including the World Tchoukball Championships (2019 & 2023), European Tchoukball Championships (2022).

At the most recent world championships the UK men's squad came 5th in the world, and at the European championships we came 3rd.

Domestically, I play for the Bury St Edmund's Kings squad who are current league champions with an impressive current run as back-to-back UK champions.

I play as a Right Shooter and am constantly looking for way to improve my game!

For more information, please see my player bio on: https://www.tchoukball.org.uk/player/jack-leitch/
